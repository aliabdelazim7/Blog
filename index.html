Lionel Messi

Lionel Messi is an Argentine professional footballer widely regarded as one of the greatest players of all time. He plays as a forward and captains both Major League Soccer club Inter Miami and the Argentina national team.

Cristiano Ronaldo

Cristiano Ronaldo is a Portuguese professional footballer who plays as a forward for and captains both Saudi Pro League club Al Nassr and the Portugal national team. Known for his incredible athleticism and goal-scoring ability.

David Beckham

David Beckham is an English former professional footballer known for his incredible crossing ability, vision, and free-kick expertise. He played as a midfielder and captained the England national team. Beckham is also famous for his time at Manchester United, Real Madrid, LA Galaxy, and Paris Saint-Germain. Beyond football, he is a global icon and philanthropist.

Fernando Torres

Fernando José Torres Sanz, commonly known as Fernando Torres, is a Spanish former professional footballer who played as a striker. Renowned for his speed, goal-scoring ability, and technical skills, Torres achieved legendary status with Liverpool and the Spain national team. He was instrumental in Spain's victories in UEFA Euro 2008, FIFA World Cup 2010, and UEFA Euro 2012.

Mohamed Salah

Mohamed Salah is an Egyptian professional footballer who plays as a forward for Premier League club Liverpool and captains the Egypt national team. Known for his incredible speed, dribbling skills, and goal-scoring ability, Salah has become one of the most feared attackers in world football. He has won numerous awards, including the Premier League Golden Boot multiple times, and was named African Footballer of the Year in 2017 and 2018.
